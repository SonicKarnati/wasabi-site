AI Empowerment Program for SHG Ecosystems 
Democratizing Intelligence and Capability for SHGs in India 
 
1. THE CORE IDEA 
There are many SHG support and empowerment programs across India covering sales channels, 
packaging, and marketing basics. But it still operates in a traditional human-effort model: teach 
a woman how to photograph her product, she does it manually. We teach her to track sales, she 
writes in a register. What Wasabi AI does differently is it embeds AI as a working partner into 
every step of that journey — not as a technology lesson, but as a daily-use tool that multiplies 
her output without multiplying her effort. 
India's SHG ecosystem is one of the world's largest women-led economic movements. Yet SHG 
women compete daily, not just with local sellers but with organized brands, D2C startups, e-
commerce platforms, and AI-assisted businesses. The gap between them is not one of quality or 
effort. It is a gap in infrastructure: access to intelligence, communication, design, and decision-
making support. 
Wasabi AI is closing that gap. 
This program trains SHG women and their ecosystem leaders to use AI as a daily business 
partner — for product development, branding, marketing, sales, pricing, and planning. It uses 
no proprietary software and introduces no new platform. Every tool taught — ChatGPT, Google 
AI, WhatsApp, voice assistants — is free, already on their phones, and available in regional 
languages today. 
The result: A woman who once needed an agency for branding, a consultant for pricing, and a 
designer for packaging can now access all of that through a voice conversation on her phone.

3. THE TRANSFORMATION JOURNEY 
Both tracks follow a deliberate progression, not a single event, but a phased capability-building 
journey with implementation gaps built in, so women practice in the real world between 
sessions and return with experience, not just memory. The program is built on a simple belief: 
“AI is not replacing women. AI is strengthening women.” 
The objective is not technology training. It is: 
• stronger decision-making, 
• greater confidence, 
• better market competitiveness, 
• and improved enterprise capability. 
The learning journey is intentionally designed to move participants through: 
Curiosity → Confidence → Application → Enterprise Transformation

Level 1 — AI Foundations & Digital Confidence (2-Day Workshop) 
The first transformation is psychological, not technical. 
• What AI is — explained through stories, analogies, and live demonstrations 
• Text AI, Voice AI, Image AI, Translation AI — introduced through play and exploration 
• Regional language voice interaction — speaking naturally, getting real answers 
• Hands-on activities: generate a product story, create a festival message, ask AI a business 
question 
• Breaking the belief: "Technology is not for me" 
• Responsible AI use: verifying outputs, privacy basics, safe digital behavior 
After Level 1: 2–3 week practice period. Participants use AI independently before returning.

7. THE BELIEF BEHIND THIS PROGRAM 
A large company has a marketing team, a designer, a pricing analyst, and a sales strategist. An 
SHG woman has herself and her phone. This program makes her phone as powerful as that 
team. 
Because the future of rural entrepreneurship will not be decided only by access to capital. 
It will increasingly be decided by: access to intelligence, execution capability, and confidence. 
SHG women are not behind because they lack intelligence or capability. They are behind 
because they have lacked infrastructure. AI is that infrastructure. This training is the bridge.
