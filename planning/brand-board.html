Image and Illustration Direction

Use
  • Real students, classrooms, workshops, and Wasabi environments.
  • People mid-action with genuine concentration, curiosity, pride, or excitement.
  • Organic masks, hand-drawn line connections, and human-centered compositions.
Avoid
  • Generic stock photography.
  • Robotic, hologram, circuit-board, or sci-fi AI visuals.
  • Busy backgrounds behind body text or technology as the hero.
Dark surface check

Forest dark supports quiet authority.

Use cream or white text. Do not use green body text on this background. Use terracotta only for one accent element when necessary.
