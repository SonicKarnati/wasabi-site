Figma file: `https://www.figma.com/design/tauCD0NcX2DAAq8eBbIKIJ`

## Motion Direction

The Figma file documents Phase 5X animation ideas as review notes, not final production requirements. Motion should remain restrained, accessible, and supportive of trust.

## Approved-To-Explore Ideas

| Interaction | Figma location | Notes |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Hero paper stack | `Desktop / Home / 1440` and `Mobile / Home / 390` | Keep as a strong first impression. Use source-derived placeholders until approved imagery exists. |
| Subtle mouse gradient | `Desktop / Home / 1440`, hero visual note | Explore gently. Must support reduced motion and should not become a constant animated background. |
| Button shine | `Components / V1 Library Map` and page CTAs | Small hover/focus flourish is acceptable for an AI site. Avoid looping or distracting shine. |
| Sticky story rhythm | `Desktop / Home / 1440`, problem section | Keep the sequence simple: axis, practice, proof. No scroll-jacking. |
| Chip/icon alignment | `Components / V1 Library Map` | Align chip centerline to icon center as requested in design feedback. |
| Proof-card spacing | `Desktop / Home / 1440`, `Desktop / Impact / 1440` | Proof labels must not crowd icons, titles, or placeholder text. |

## Reduced Motion Requirements

- Animated gradients should become static backgrounds.
- Button shine should be disabled or reduced to a simple color/fill transition.
- Sticky sections should remain readable as normal stacked content.
- No content should depend on animation to become visible.

## Not Approved

- Scroll-jacking.
- Constant futuristic glow effects.
- Fake AI hologram or sci-fi imagery.
- Animated counters for unverified metrics.
- Motion that hides placeholder or verification labels.
