## 5. Visual Decisions Reflected

- Deep forest, wasabi green, warm cream, terracotta, ink, and muted neutral colors are represented.
- The design keeps the premium forest/cream direction from the experimental branch.
- The home hero includes the paper-stack visual idea the reviewer liked.
- The chip/icon alignment issue is documented in the component map.
- Button shine is documented as a restrained hover/focus flourish.
- The proof/credibility sections keep placeholder labels visible.

## 6. Known Limitations

- Figma Starter plan limited the file to three page tabs.
- Raster assets were not uploaded into Figma; source visuals are represented as editable placeholders.
- Figma uses Inter as an editable fallback; production typography remains Cormorant Garamond and DM Sans.
- This is a design translation and review artifact, not final visual approval.
