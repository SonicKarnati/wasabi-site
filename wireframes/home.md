# Home Page Wireframe

Purpose: Establish what Wasabi is, why it matters, what it does, and why serious visitors should trust it.

Audience: Government visitors, institutional partners, schools, parents, international visitors, and general public.

Primary CTA: Partner with Wasabi

Secondary CTA: Explore Programs

Review questions:

- Does this page establish credibility quickly?
- Does it explain Wasabi without sounding generic?
- Does it keep unverified proof clearly marked?
- Does the CTA fit government and institutional visitors?
