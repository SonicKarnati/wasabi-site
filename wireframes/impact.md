# Impact Page Wireframe

Purpose: Build trust by showing what Wasabi can prove, what is planned, and what needs verification.

Audience: Government, institutional partners, sponsors, international visitors, schools, and media.

Primary CTA: Request Impact Details

Secondary CTA: Partner on a Pilot

Review questions:

- Which metrics are verified and approved?
- Does the page avoid turning projected scale into achieved impact?
- What case studies can be used with consent?
- What proof would make this credible for government/institutional visitors?
